digger.level

cleanup

(cleanup level {x :x, y :y})

Removes the items at x y

create

(create [width height :as size] [diamond-pct bag-pct dug-pct :as percentages])

Generate a new level with a specific width and height. Diamonds, bags and dug cells percetages are also specified and shouldn’t total above 100, friendly advice.

dig

(dig level {x :x, y :y})

Add a :dug keyword to cell at x y to mark it dug.

draw

(draw level sprite-map [w h])

Draw each cell and items of the level with its according sprite.

index-level

(index-level level)

items-at

(items-at level {x :x, y :y})

Retrieves the items located at x y

print

(print level)

Print the given level, with ’*‘ as diamonds, ‘O’ as bags, a space as dug cells and ’-’ as undug cells.
